title: Beyond Compare
---

1. 进入文件夹`~/Library/Application Support/Beyond Compare`
2. 修改`BCState.xml`:

  ```
  <TCheckForUpdatesState>
      <Build Value="24545"/>
  </TCheckForUpdatesState>
  ```

3. 修改`BCSessions.xml`

  ```
  <BCSessions Version="1" MinVersion="1">
  </BCSessions>
```
